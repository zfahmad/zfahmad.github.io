About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Alberta working with Nathan Sturtevant. I am passionate about research in artificial intelligence --- my work focuses on research in machine learning, search, reinforcement learning, games and planning. I previously received my Ph.D. at the Department of Computing Science at the University of Alberta. I worked under the co-supervision of Michael Bowling and Levi Lelis. Prior to that, I completed my Master's at the University of Alberta under the supervision of Robert Holte. I received my Bachelor's of Sciences and Engineering at the Islamic University of Technology in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
